Whether you're looking for local volunteer opportunities, virtual projects, or long-term commitments, we provide a seamless way to find and engage with causes that matter to you.For NGOs, we offer a platform to share opportunities, reach dedicated volunteers, and grow their mission with the right support. Our Mission

At ImpactTrail,

our mission is to bridge the gap between passionate volunteers and organizations in need. We believe in creating a world where every individual can contribute to meaningful causes and make a difference. By connecting volunteers with NGOs and nonprofits, we empower communities and drive positive change.


Featured Organizations



Kshitij Foundation

The Kshitij Foundation is a youth-led Indian organization promoting menstrual hygiene awareness and education through campaigns and events, aiming for healthy, safe, and educated lives for girls.

The Golden Estate

The Golden Estate is a premium senior living facility in Delhi NCR, India, offering assisted living with 24/7 medical support, recreational activities, and a luxurious, secure environment for a fulfilling lifestyle.

Pratham

The Pratham Foundation is one of India's largest NGOs, focusing on improving education quality for underprivileged children through low-cost, replicable models and conducting the Annual Status of Education Report (ASER).

Goonj

The Goonj Foundation is a non-profit organization in India that uses urban surplus material to address rural development needs, promoting dignity and self-worth by engaging communities in public work projects

Grow Billion Trees

The Grow a Billion Trees initiative aims to plant and protect billions of trees globally to combat climate change, improve the environment, and enhance local communities. They focus on native species and work with local communities for sustainable, impactful projects

India Water Foundation

The India Water Foundation is a non-profit organization promoting public awareness about water's vital role in daily life, health, and economic growth. They focus on sustainable development, community resilience, and environmental conservation in the Asia-Pacific region.

Shield Foundation

The Shield Foundation is a Mumbai-based organization focused on providing healthcare, education, vocational training, and recreational services to disadvantaged populations, especially senior citizens in Dharavi. Their mission is to promote physical and mental well-being and foster equal growth opportunities for all.

Hand In Hand

The Hand in Hand Foundation is a non-profit organization focused on providing permanence in children's lives through adoption, education, and therapeutic services. They operate locally in California and support family-style orphanages globally, aiming to create lasting positive change for children in need.

Smile Foundation

The Smile Foundation is an Indian NGO impacting over 15 lakh children and families annually through education, healthcare, livelihood, and women empowerment projects in remote villages and urban slums across 25 states

HelpAge India

The HelpAge India Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the quality of life for disadvantaged older persons. Established in 1978, they focus on healthcare, livelihood, disaster response, and advocacy for the elderly, aiming to create a society where elders live active, healthy, and dignified lives
